Thank you for installing Mukti v2!

Mukti is now registered with Microsoft Office.
Next time you open Word, Excel, or PowerPoint, look for the
"Mukti" tab in the ribbon at the top.

Click the Mukti button to open the conversion panel.
Then: Scan the document, review the changes, and click Apply.

If the Mukti tab does not appear:
  Go to File -> Options -> Add-ins
  Set "Manage" to "COM Add-ins" and click Go
  Tick the checkbox next to "Mukti" and click OK

For help: https://github.com/GRU-953/Mukti
